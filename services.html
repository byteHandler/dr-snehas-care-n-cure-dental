Our Services

Comprehensive dental care for every member of your family

Home Services

Routine Dental Care

Regular dental care is the foundation of a healthy smile. Our comprehensive routine care services help prevent dental problems and maintain optimal oral health for you and your family.

Routine Checkup

Comprehensive dental examination to assess your oral health, identify potential issues early, and create a personalized treatment plan.

Dental Scaling & Polishing

Professional cleaning to remove plaque, tartar, and stains, leaving your teeth clean, smooth, and refreshed.

Tooth X-Ray

Digital dental X-rays for accurate diagnosis of hidden dental problems, cavities, and bone-related issues.

Tooth-Colored Fillings

Natural-looking composite fillings that match your tooth color, restoring both function and aesthetics of damaged teeth.

Root Canal Treatment

Save your natural tooth with our expert root canal treatments. Using advanced techniques, we ensure a comfortable and virtually painless procedure to relieve tooth pain and prevent extraction.

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Precise removal of infected pulp tissue, thorough cleaning, and sealing of root canals to save your natural tooth and eliminate pain.

Single Sitting Root Canal Treatment

Complete your root canal in just one visit with our advanced single-sitting technique, saving you time while delivering excellent results.

Crowns, Bridges & Dentures

Restore your smile with precision-crafted dental prosthetics. Whether you need to protect a damaged tooth or replace missing ones, we offer durable, natural-looking solutions.

Dental Crowns

Custom-made caps that cover and protect damaged or weakened teeth, restoring their shape, size, strength, and appearance.

Dental Bridges

Fixed prosthetic devices that bridge the gap created by missing teeth, anchored to adjacent natural teeth for stability.

Dentures

Removable replacements for missing teeth, custom-fitted for comfort and designed to look and function like natural teeth.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhance the beauty of your smile with our advanced cosmetic dental treatments. From whitening to complete smile makeovers, we help you achieve the smile you've always wanted.

Teeth Whitening / Bleaching

Professional teeth whitening treatments to remove stains and discoloration, giving you a brighter, more radiant smile.

Dental Veneers

Thin, custom-made shells bonded to the front of your teeth to improve their appearance, covering chips, gaps, and discoloration.

Smile Designing

Comprehensive smile makeover combining multiple cosmetic procedures to create your perfect, natural-looking smile.

Dental Implants

The gold standard for tooth replacement. Dental implants provide a permanent, natural-looking solution for missing teeth with the strength and feel of your own natural teeth.

Single Tooth Implants

Replace a single missing tooth with a titanium implant and custom crown that looks, feels, and functions like a natural tooth.

Multiple Tooth Implants

Replace several missing teeth with strategically placed implants supporting bridges or dentures for comprehensive restoration.

Orthodontics

Achieve perfectly aligned teeth with our orthodontic treatments. We offer solutions to correct misaligned teeth, bite issues, and spacing problems for a straighter, healthier smile.

Orthodontic Treatment

Comprehensive orthodontic care to correct crooked teeth, overcrowding, gaps, and bite irregularities for an improved smile and better oral health.

Tooth Extraction

When a tooth cannot be saved, our gentle extraction procedures ensure minimal discomfort and quick recovery. We prioritize your comfort throughout the process.

Simple Tooth Extraction

Gentle removal of damaged or decayed teeth that cannot be restored, performed with proper anesthesia for a comfortable experience.

Third Molar / Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Expert removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth to prevent pain, infection, and damage to surrounding teeth.

Kids Dentistry

Creating positive dental experiences for children. Our child-friendly approach helps build healthy dental habits early, ensuring your child's smile develops beautifully.

Pediatric Dental Care

Gentle, age-appropriate dental treatments for children including checkups, cleanings, fluoride treatments, and dental sealants to protect young teeth.

Dental Education for Kids

Teaching children proper brushing and flossing techniques, and educating parents on maintaining their child's oral health at home.
